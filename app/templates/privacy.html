Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 5/22/2024

Welcome to Thyme! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our web app, Thyme, which helps users create and read events from their Google Calendars through an AI-powered chat interface.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

When you use Thyme, we may collect personal information that can identify you, such as:

  • Email Address, Name, & Profile Photo: Collected through Google OAuth 2.0 Authentication.
  • Google Calendar Data: Events and related information from your Google Calendar.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app.

b. Non-Personal Information

We may also collect non-personal information, which cannot be used to identify you, including but not limited to:

  • Browser and Device Information: Type of device, browser, and operating system.
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, and pages viewed.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in the following ways:

  • To Provide Services: Using your Google Calendar data to create and read events via the AI chat interface.
  • To Improve Our Services: Analyzing usage data to enhance app functionality and user experience.
  • To Communicate with You: Sending updates, security alerts, and support messages.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third parties that provide services on our behalf, such as Heroku (hosting) and Porkbun (domain management).
  • OpenAI API: For processing natural language queries.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no security measures are perfect, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

5. Your Choices

a. Access and Update Information

You can access and update your personal information through your Google account settings.

b. Revoke Permissions

You can revoke Thyme's access to your Google Calendar and other permissions through your Google account settings at any time.

6. Third-Party Links

Thyme may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not operated by us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these third-party sites.

7. Children's Privacy

Thyme is not intended for use by children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
